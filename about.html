About Me

UI Designer with the urge to deliver user-friendly products by intuitive and inspiring design with a focus on user needs.

It has always been natural to me to put myself in the shoes of others, empathize and find solutions to solve problems. My background within the hospitality industry has taught me to feel in charge of executing tasks and I developed a high-level customer-oriented behaviour. During the past three years working in account management, I learned to adapt quickly and efficiently to my work environment nurturing the relationship to clients, supporting them and keeping the focus on business growth. Combining both my account management experience and my visual sense for design, I want to design intuitive and inspiring products for a positive user experience.
